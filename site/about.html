Growing from seeds of jazz, soul, rock, folk, showtunes and film music, Misses Ellipses is a hunt for the magical power of a song to tell a compelling story and reveal moods and ideas shrouded in mystery.

Amanda is currently studying experimental music at California Institute of the Arts.
